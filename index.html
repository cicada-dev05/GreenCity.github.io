North Eastern India

North-East India is one of the most fascinating places of India and of the world, located at the northeastern region of India, and consisting of 7 states, called collectively as 'the seven sisters'. Much of the region has been left unexplored till date, owning to the regions formidable terrain consisting of mountains, hills, rainforests, river, jungles and predators, and deficiency of infrastructure. The small sized region has one of the highest biodiversity density found anywhere in the world.

Popular in North Eastern

North East home to various cultural and tribal diversties of India with a strong essense of lush-green enivroment.

Guwahati, Assam

Guwahati is the gateway to the Northeast, besides being one of the largest cities of this region. There are several opportunities for sightseeing in this city.

Best time to visit: Oct-Apr

Kaziranga National Park, Assam

Kaziranga National Park in Assam, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is renowned for being home to the one-horned rhino. It’s also one of the most amazing holiday destinations in Northeast India.

Best time to visit: Nov-Apr

Majuli, Assam

Majuli is a river island in Assam that can only be accessed through a ferry ride from Jorhat. Majuli is special for so many reasons, with one of them being that it is the first district island in the country.

Best time to visit: Oct-Feb

Aizwal, Mizoram

Aizwal, the capital of Mizoram and an immensely beautiful hill city, provides the best sightseeing opportunities. You will find solitude and tranquility in abundance here.

Best time to visit: Mar-Jul

Shillong, Meghalaya

Surrounded by hills and the ever present fog resting comfortably on them, Shillong appears like the perfect getaway for a dreamy vacation.

Best time to visit: Mar-May

Mawsynram, Meghalaya

Mawsynram, located about 56km from Shillong, is a tiny village in the Khasi village of Meghalaya. It is famous for being the wettest place on earth, along with Cherrapunji.

Best time to visit: Sep-Nov

Sivasagar, Assam

You will enjoy your visit to Sivasagar especially if you are a history enthusiast. It is home to several places of immense historical importance.

Best time to visit: Oct-Feb

Manas Tiger Reserve

It is most famous for its enormous population of tigers. Alongside, this tiger reserve also boasts the largest population of Bengal Florican in the world, besides being an incredible place to watch the Great Hornbill.

Best time to visit: Oct-May

Tawang,Arurnachal Pradesh

The smallest district of Arunachal Pradesh, its home to several attractions including Tawang Monastery, Nuranang Waterfalls, Taktsang Gompa, Pankang Teng Tso Lake and Jaswant Garh, besides others.

Best time to visit:Mar-Jun / Sep-Oct

Bomdila Arunachal Pradesh

Bomdila is one of the most incredible destinations of Arunachal Pradesh because of its lush landscape combined with a rich culture and heritage.

Best time to visit: Apr-Oct

Pelling, Sikkim

Pelling is one of the most popular tourist attractions in the Northeast because it offers breathtaking views of the Khangchendzonga and surrounding ranges.

Best time to visit: Feb-May / Sep or Dec

Mawsynram, Meghalaya

Mawsynram, located about 56km from Shillong, is a tiny village in the Khasi village of Meghalaya. It is famous for being the wettest place on earth, along with Cherrapunji.

Best time to visit:Sep-Nov
